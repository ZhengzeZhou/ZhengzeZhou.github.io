%% ── About ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{About}

I am currently a Staff AI Engineer at LinkedIn Core AI. Previously, I was a Research Scientist
at Meta Platforms, where I worked on recommendation systems and search ranking.
I received my PhD in Statistics from Cornell University under the supervision of Giles Hooker.
My current research and engineering focus is on LLM post-training, reinforcement learning
for language models, inference efficiency, and LLM-powered generative recommendation systems.
