About Me

  • Staff AI Engineer at LinkedIn Core AI, working on LLM post-training, inference efficiency, and LLM for ranking and generative recommendations.
  • Previously I was a Research Scientist at Meta, working on recommendation systems and contextual search.
  • I received my PhD in Statistics from Cornell University under the supervision of Giles Hooker, with research focused on interpretable machine learning and uncertainty quantification.
  • Before Cornell, I received my BS in Probability and Statistics from Peking University.
